## Digital Advertising Market Size & TAM

### TAM Estimate 1: WARC (Global Advertising)
- Global total advertising spend 2025: $1.17 trillion
- Global total advertising spend 2026 forecast: $1.27 trillion (8.5% growth)
- Social media ad spend 2025: $306.4 billion (+14.9% YoY, WARC)
- Social media ad spend 2026 forecast: ~$350+ billion
- Digital % of total ad spend: >75% in 2025 (first time)
- Source: WARC Global Ad Forecast, 2025; WARC The Big Picture: Social Media 2025

### TAM Estimate 2: WPP Media (formerly GroupM)
- Total global ad revenue 2025: $1.14 trillion (+8.8%)
- Total global ad revenue 2026 forecast: +7.1% growth
- Source: WPP Media Global Ad Forecast, December 2025

### TAM Estimate 3: Precedence Research (Digital Ad Spending)
- Digital ad spending market 2025: $650 billion
- Digital ad spending market 2026 forecast: $710 billion
- 2026-2035 CAGR: 9.38%
- Source: Precedence Research

### TAM Estimate 4: Social Media Advertising (Independent)
- Social media advertising market 2025: $202.63 billion
- Social media advertising market 2026: $227.95 billion
- Social media advertising market 2030: $376.92 billion
- 2026-2030 CAGR: 13.4%
- Source: Independent market research via xtendedview.com

### Key TAM Reconciliation Note
Wide variation in TAM estimates due to differing scope (total media vs. digital only vs. social only). Consistent finding: double-digit CAGR in social advertising through at least 2030. Meta's 2025 revenue of $200.97B represents ~17.7% of global digital ad spend (~$310B in social context).

## Key Competitors

### Google/Alphabet (GOOGL)
- YouTube ad revenue 2024: $33+ billion
- Combined Google/YouTube ad market leadership: ~#1 globally
- Competition: Search ads (no direct overlap with Meta), YouTube vs. Reels, AI assistant (Gemini vs. Meta AI)
- Note: Google and Meta colluded in ad market per Jedi Blue settlement (2021)

### TikTok (ByteDance)
- TikTok ad revenue 2025 forecast: $33.1 billion (+40.5% YoY)
- TikTok global digital ad share: ~5%
- Competitive threat: Short video, younger demographics, Reels competitor
- US status: TikTok ban/divestiture legal situation ongoing as of early 2026
- Meta/Google stood to gain share from TikTok ban (per Axios Jan 2025)

### Snap Inc. (SNAP)
- Much smaller (~$5B revenue); competes on younger demographics
- Less direct competitive threat at scale

### Amazon (AMZN)
- Amazon ad revenue growing rapidly (~$60B+ run rate 2024)
- Retail media competition emerging; less direct overlap with social

### Apple (AAPL)
- ATT (App Tracking Transparency) impact: ~$10B headwind to Meta 2022-2023
- Competition in AI assistant space (Siri, Apple Intelligence)
- No direct advertising overlap but significant platform dependency risk

### Microsoft/LinkedIn
- B2B advertising; minimal overlap with Meta's B2C focus

## Advertising Market Cyclicality
- Digital advertising typically correlates with GDP growth (0.8-1.2x multiplier)
- Recession risk: Digital ad spend can decline 15-25% in downturns (2022 Meta saw -1% revenue)
- Digital ad recovery post-2022: Strong, accelerating through AI-driven efficiency
