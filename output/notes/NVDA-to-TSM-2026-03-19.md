## Observation

## Implication for TSMC

NVIDIA's demand concentration at TSMC is a double-edged sword for TSMC investors: (1) Revenue visibility is exceptional — NVIDIA's backlog and roadmap make TSMC the primary beneficiary of the AI compute buildout through 2028+; (2) Customer concentration risk — NVIDIA approaching 20-25% of TSMC total revenue creates single-customer dependency; (3) CoWoS capacity expansion investment (2-3x by 2028) is being driven by NVIDIA demand but will eventually erode NVIDIA's bottleneck pricing power (see NVDA industry analysis).

TSMC's announced CoWoS expansion is critical to the AI infrastructure buildout timeline. Delays in CoWoS expansion would sustain NVIDIA's supply constraint pricing power longer but also cap NVDA revenue growth. TSMC CoWoS execution is a key leading indicator for NVDA FY2028+ revenue trajectory.
