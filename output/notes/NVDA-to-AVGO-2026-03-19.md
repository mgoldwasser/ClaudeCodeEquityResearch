## Observation

Two dynamics from the NVDA industry analysis are relevant to Broadcom: (1) Custom ASIC shipments are growing at 44.6% in 2026 vs. GPU growth of 16.1% (Bloomberg Intelligence). Broadcom is the primary custom ASIC designer for hyperscaler AI programs (Google TPU components, Meta MTIA, others). The ASIC growth rate directly benefits Broadcom's custom silicon segment. (2) NVIDIA announced at GTC 2026 an expanded networking stack (NVLink 6, ConnectX-9 SuperNIC, Spectrum-6 Ethernet) — these networking products compete directly with Broadcom's AI cluster interconnect and switch silicon in hyperscaler data centers.

## Implication for Broadcom

The custom ASIC buildout (44.6% growth) is a structural tailwind for AVGO's AI custom silicon business. Each incremental hyperscaler AI program that goes "custom" rather than buying NVIDIA GPUs likely goes through Broadcom for silicon design. The risk is NVIDIA's networking expansion — as NVDA integrates NVLink into full-rack architectures, it displaces standalone networking silicon purchases from Broadcom in NVIDIA-heavy clusters. Net assessment: AVGO benefits from ASIC growth; faces incremental competition in networking from NVIDIA's vertical integration.
