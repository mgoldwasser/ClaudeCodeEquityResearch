## 1. COMPANY OVERVIEW

**NVIDIA Corporation** designs and manufactures graphics processing units (GPUs) and system-on-chip units for the gaming, professional visualization, data center, and automotive markets. The company operates primarily as a fabless semiconductor company — all manufacturing is outsourced to TSMC.

NVIDIA's business has undergone a fundamental transformation: in FY2026, Data Center accounted for 89.7% of revenue ($193.7B of $215.9B total), driven almost entirely by demand for AI accelerators (training and inference for large language models and AI applications).

## 5. COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE

### Market Share (AI Accelerators)

| Company | Revenue Share 2024 | Share 2026E | Trend |
|---------|-------------------|------------|-------|
| NVIDIA | ~87–90% | ~75–80% | Declining but dominant |
| AMD | ~5–7% | ~8–12% | Growing |
| Intel | ~2–3% | ~2–3% | Stagnant |
| Custom ASICs | ~5–8% | ~10–15% | Growing fast |

### NVIDIA Data Center vs. Competitors (Scale)
- NVIDIA Q4 FY2026 DC revenue: $62.3B/quarter
- AMD quarterly DC revenue: ~$4.3B (Q3 2025)
- Intel DCAI quarterly revenue: ~$4B
- **NVIDIA is 7x larger than AMD + Intel combined in AI compute**

### CUDA Moat
- 20-year head start; 4M+ developers; 3,000+ optimized applications
- PyTorch, TensorFlow, JAX all natively optimized for CUDA
- Switching cost: months of engineering + $100K+ to migrate to AMD ROCm
- Emerging threat: OpenAI Triton (hardware-agnostic compiler) — adoption growing but early

### Supply Chain Competitive Position
- NVIDIA holds priority allocation for CoWoS packaging at TSMC (800K-850K wafers booked 2026)
- >60% of global HBM consumption (Morgan Stanley)
- HBM3E fully allocated through 2026; HBM4 supply for Vera Rubin secured
- AMD is secondary CoWoS recipient — supply-constrained by NVIDIA's dominance

### Key Risks to Competitive Position
1. Custom silicon from hyperscalers (Google TPU, Amazon Trainium) — reduces NVIDIA TAM at hyperscaler layer
2. AMD MI400 series gaining ground in cost-sensitive inference workloads
3. Triton/hardware-agnostic compilers slowly eroding CUDA exclusivity
4. China domestic AI chip development accelerating (Huawei Ascend) — domestic market share loss
