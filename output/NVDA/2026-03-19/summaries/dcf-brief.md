# DCF Brief — NVIDIA (NVDA)

**Analyst:** DCF Analyst

**Headline Finding:** NVIDIA's DCF fair value ranges $62.90-$265.41 across bear/base/bull cases with probability-weighted expected value of $159.54/share; terminal value dominates 68-84% of enterprise value across all scenarios, making the investment thesis entirely dependent on long-run competitive position assumptions, not near-term earnings visibility.

**Key Numbers:**
- Base case fair value: $165.14/share
- Bull case: $265.41/share (25% probability)
- Bear case: $62.90/share (30% probability)
- Probability-weighted expected value: $159.54/share
- FY2027 revenue guidance: $78.0B (base case implies $306.6B annualized)
- Terminal revenue (FY2031, base): $582.5B (vs. $215.9B FY2026)
- Terminal gross margin (base): 73.0% (vs. FY2026 71.1%)
- WACC base case: 14.0% (bull: 13.0%, bear: 15.5%)
- Terminal growth rate: 2.5% (bull: 3.5%, bear: 2.0%)
- Terminal EBITDA multiple (base, 50/50 GGM/Exit): 22x
- FCF FY2027 (base): $152.3B; FY2031: $283.1B

- [ASSUMPTION: Gross margin sustained at 73% terminal (vs. FY26 71.1%) despite AMD competition and ASIC growth — implies CUDA moat protection through FY2031]
- [ASSUMPTION: WACC 14% reflects beta 2.0, ERP 4.6%, Rf 4.4% + 1% company-specific risk; elevated beta captures customer concentration and China regulatory risk]
- [ASSUMPTION: Terminal EBITDA multiple 22x represents blended semi (15x) and software (30x) valuation — justified by CUDA ecosystem but creates high sensitivity to competitive positioning]
