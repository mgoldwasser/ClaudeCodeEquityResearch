## PRICE-BLINDED — Director of Research

NVIDIA is the dominant supplier of AI accelerator chips with 86-92% market share in data center GPUs. FY2026 revenue reached $215.9B (+65% YoY), with Q4 revenue of $68.1B and Q1 FY2027 guidance of $78.0B (±2%), beating consensus by 7.4%.
