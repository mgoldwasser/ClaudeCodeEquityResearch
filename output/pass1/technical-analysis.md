**Alignment summary:**
The technicals diverge meaningfully from the fundamental consensus. The sell-side consensus Buy rating with $261 average price target implies 36% upside, but the technical picture shows a stock in a confirmed downtrend, trading below all major moving averages, with a broken $200 support level and a distribution pattern. This divergence is not unusual for a stock where the market is repricing execution risk (MI450 delays, CUDA gap, mega-deal dilution) faster than sell-side models are adjusting. A disciplined approach would be to establish a starter position at current levels and add aggressively only if: (1) price stabilizes above $185 and forms a base, (2) RSI reaches genuine oversold territory (<25) on a capitulation volume spike, or (3) the stock reclaims the 200-day MA (~$228) on expanding volume, confirming the downtrend has ended.

## Technical Summary

AMD is in a confirmed medium-term downtrend, having declined ~28% from its October 2025 high of $267.08 to ~$192 as of early March 2026. The stock trades below its 20-day ($202), 200-day ($228), and 50-day ($242) moving averages -- a fully bearish MA alignment. Key near-term support sits at $185-191 (accumulated volume and prior resistance-turned-support), with major structural support at $165 (2021 highs). Nearest resistance is the broken $200 level. RSI at ~32-39 is approaching but has not reached oversold extremes. The February earnings reaction (-17% on high volume) confirmed institutional distribution. Current conditions represent a **Neutral to Poor** entry for a fundamental buyer: the stock is technically damaged in the near term, but approaching oversold conditions near volume support. The technicals diverge from the fundamental consensus (Buy, $261 target) and argue for patience, scaled entry, and stop discipline rather than aggressive accumulation. For a fundamental bull, the $165-185 zone would represent a high-conviction technical entry. At current levels (~$192), only a starter position (1/3 or less) is technically justified, with the expectation of adding on either a confirmed reversal or a deeper washout to stronger support.
