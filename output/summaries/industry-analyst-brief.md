# Industry Analyst — Structured Brief

## Bottom Line
NVIDIA dominates AI accelerator market at 86-92% market share (2024-2025) in rapidly expanding $116B sector (2024) projected to grow 25-40% CAGR to $295-500B by 2030. CUDA ecosystem moat is durable but eroding; base case forecasts NVIDIA share decline to 63-75% by 2030. TAM expansion offsets volume share loss, enabling continued revenue growth to $400-500B (2030) despite share compression.

## Key Numbers
- **Current TAM (2024):** $116B; **2030E:** $295-500B (linear case: $734B at 36% CAGR)
- **Current NVIDIA share:** 92% (2024); **2030E:** 63-83% base case (linear model); 62% S-curve model; Monte Carlo mean 63.6% (±10th-90th percentile: 61.2%-66.1%)
- **Moat score:** 6.0/10 overall (CUDA switching costs 8/10; cost advantage 4/10; network effects 6/10; brand 6/10; IP 6/10)
- **Gross margin (current):** 75%; **2030E:** 72% (mix shift to inference -200bps, pricing pressure -100bps)
- **NVIDIA revenue 2030:** Base case linear 83% share × 72% margin × $734B TAM = $436B gross profit; S-curve 63% share × 72% margin × $735B TAM = $334B gross profit
- **Custom silicon threat:** 65% probability reaches 15-20% market share by 2030; revenue impact -$25-50B
- **AMD threat:** 55% probability MI400 series gains 12-18% share by 2030; revenue impact -$15-25B
- **Market concentration (HHI):** 8,493 (highly concentrated); CR4: 99.5%

## Critical Assumptions
- AI capex cycle extends through end of 2027 with gradual cooldown post-2028 (risk: cliff if targets met early)
- Custom silicon remains primarily internal-only (probability 65%); if monetized externally, NVIDIA share loss accelerates +300-500bps
- AMD ROCm ecosystem lag compresses from 2-3 years (current) to 1-2 years by 2027 (50% probability)
- TSMC CoWoS packaging is binding constraint through 2027; relief Q4 2026-Q1 2027
- HBM3E/HBM4 supply co-constrained with CoWoS; prices rising 15-20% YoY through 2026
- Hyperscaler capex: $400B+ committed through 2026-2027 (Google $100B+, Amazon $100B+, Microsoft $100B+, Meta $60B+)

## Key Risk to Thesis
**Custom silicon hyperscaler expansion:** If Google/Amazon/Meta accelerate external monetization of TPU/Trainium/MTIA to tier-2 cloud providers and AI startups (probability 65% by 2030), NVIDIA's TAM shrinks $25-50B and share compresses to 55-60% instead of 65-75% base case. Moat erodes faster than modeled if PyTorch 2.0/TensorFlow achieve true vendor-neutrality by 2027 (20-25% probability), collapsing CUDA switching costs from $5M to $1-2M.
