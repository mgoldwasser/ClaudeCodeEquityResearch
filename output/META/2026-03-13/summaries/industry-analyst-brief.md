# Industry Analyst Brief — META
## Date: 2026-03-13

### Bottom Line
**Constructive on digital advertising secular tailwind.** META controls 64% of social media ad market ($306B global) and ~13.8% of all digital advertising. Growth is constrained by law of large numbers (market penetration ~64% social share), not competitive displacement. Five of seven Helmer Powers (Network Effects, Switching Costs, Scale Economies, Data, Process Power) rated 7-9/10; Power Durability Timeline shows moat resilience through 2030 if AI competitiveness maintained.

### Key Numbers
- **TAM estimates:**  Global ad spend $1.14-1.17T (+8.5-8.8%); digital $650B (+8-9%); social media $306B (+13-15% CAGR)
- **META market share:** ~64% of social ads globally; ~13.8% of all digital advertising
- **Helmer 7 Powers assessment:** Network Effects (9/10), Switching Costs (8/10), Scale Economies (8/10), Data (9/10), Process Power (7/10), Brand (6/10), Regulatory Licenses (3/10 — EU DMA constraint)
- **Financial Translation Matrix:** Scale Economies → +3pp gross margin vs. peers; Data → pricing power (+1-2pp relative ARPU); Network Effects → land grab resilience
- **Power Durability:** Network Effects have 20+ year half-life; Data/Scale 5-10 years if AI moat maintained; Brand declining post-DMA/content moderation changes
- **Competitive Vulnerability:** vs. TikTok: TikTok weaker on scale but stronger on algorithm; vs. GOOGL: YouTube 5% ad share but different workload (intent vs. attention); vs. SNAP: SNAP 2% share, undifferentiated
- **Ad revenue drivers:** Impression growth +12% (YoY), price/ad growth +9%, Advantage+ run rate $60B, Reels >50% of Instagram time
- **Revenue deceleration:** 22% (FY2025) → consensus 27% (FY2026) → projected 15-20% by FY2027-28
